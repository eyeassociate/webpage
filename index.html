About EYE Associate

Connecting trade globally with trust and reliability.

Established in 2005, EYE Associate specializes in global Import & Export operations. From shipping containers to air cargo, we handle documentation, customs clearing, freight forwarding, and secure warehousing.

With offices and warehouses in Turkey, China, and the USA, we simplify global trade and ensure smooth cargo movement for our clients.
